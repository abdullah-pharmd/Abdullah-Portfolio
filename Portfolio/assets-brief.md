### 3. Profile / About Photo
**Where used:** About section, hero sidebar (once added)  
**Aspect Ratio:** `3:4` (portrait)  
**Size:** `600 × 800 px`  
**Note:** Use your REAL photo here — AI faces look fake and hurt trust.

**Shot checklist for your real photo:**
- Dark or neutral background (plain wall, dark fabric)
- Soft natural window light from one side
- Look at camera, slight forward lean = confidence
- Smart casual — no suit needed, no hoodie either
- Crop tight: head + shoulders only
- Edit: bump contrast slightly, cool color tone matches the site
